Title: Statement on the International Criminal Court Arrest Warrant Applications for Senior Israeli Officials
URL: https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/documents/statement-the-international-criminal-court-arrest-warrant-applications-for-senior-israeli

The ICC Prosecutor's application for arrest warrants against Israeli leaders is outrageous. And let me be clear: whatever this Prosecutor might imply, there is no equivalence—none—between Israel and Hamas. We will always stand with Israel against threats to its security.
